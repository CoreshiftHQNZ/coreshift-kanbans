Velocity

An agentic sales operating system for Growth Partners.

Runs the full deal lifecycle — Prospect → Engaged → Qualified → Presented → Verbal → Won — with stage gates that are a test and a gift, an extractor that auto-populates the deal from notes/transcripts/URLs, and a buyer portal that keeps the client involved. V1.0 is live in production; the V1.1 sales-cycle + portal-interactivity batch is shipped to staging and waiting on a production promotion.
