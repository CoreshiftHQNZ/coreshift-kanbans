HeyGem

Remote admin support for Kiwi tradies — “put a Gem on it.”

A four-surface platform for a tradie remote-admin service: public marketing site, Stripe checkout, a customer dashboard showing “what your Gem sorted this week,” and an internal staff/ops workspace where pooled “Gems” log their work and minutes. Those logged minutes are the single source of truth for both the dashboard and weekly metered billing.

Current phase Phase 1 · Marketing site
Next milestone Marketing live on heygem.co.nz needs Railway deploy + Cloudflare DNS

The Three Goals

💸
Get tradies paid faster
Same-day invoicing + chasing, surfaced back to the customer as real weekly results.
⏱️
Minutes are the source of truth
Gems log time per task; the same data feeds the dashboard and the Stripe metered bill. One pipeline, no reconciliation.
💎
Every assistant is “Gem”
A pooled team all present as one branded persona — so customers say “hey Gem, thanks Gem, you’re a gem.”
