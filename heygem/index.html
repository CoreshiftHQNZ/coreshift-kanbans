HeyGem

Concierge & errands for Kiwi small businesses — “put a Gem on it.”

Chat-first concierge service: a business texts their Gem (“can you organise team morning tea for Friday?”), the Gem quotes in the chat, the customer approves, the Gem sorts it, and the saved card is charged on completion — followed by a push notification for a star rating, feedback and a photo. One React codebase ships the customer experience as a web app AND native iOS/Android apps via Capacitor; Gems work from a web workspace.

Current phase Phase R3 · Gem workspace
Next milestone Gem workspace (claim → quote → complete) Phase R3

The Three Goals

💬
Text your Gem, it's sorted
Every request is a chat thread per job with auto status and archiving — quote, approve, done, rated.
💳
Charged only when it's done
All-in quote approved in chat; saved card charged off-session on completion, GST receipt sent.
📱
One codebase, every platform
React + Capacitor ships web, iOS and Android from the same app — native push for quotes, completion and ratings.
