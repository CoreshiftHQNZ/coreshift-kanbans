KeyContent

AI content creation SaaS for agencies and their clients.

KeyContent is a multi-tenant platform where agencies manage clients, generate AI content (blog, social video, social image), schedule and publish via Zernio, and share a client review portal — all built on React + Express + Supabase, hosted on Railway, and monitored by Sentinel.

Current phase SHIP · Live in Production
Next milestone Client reactivation UI This week

The Three Goals

🎯
Content without the agency overhead
Agencies deliver AI-generated blog posts, social video, and social images to clients — from brief to publish, in one platform.
⚡
One platform, every client
Multi-agency, multi-client architecture with role-based access, a client review portal, and calendar scheduling built in.
🌐
Observable and operationally sound
Sentry, Better Stack, Sentinel, Postmark, and Zernio webhooks keep the platform visible and recoverable around the clock.
